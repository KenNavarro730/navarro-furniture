What We Take (and Don't Take)

Be upfront and we'll give you a quick answer

‚úÖ What We Take

  • Sofas, sectionals, loveseats in good condition
  • Clean upholstery (stains we can treat are OK)
  • Sturdy frames with no major damage
  • Pet-friendly homes (we deep clean everything)
  • Leather, fabric, velvet, microfiber
  • Mid-century, modern, traditional styles
  • Designer brands (West Elm, CB2, Pottery Barn, etc.)

‚ùå What We Don't Take

  • Bed bugs or any pest infestation
  • Heavy smoke odor (cigarette/fire damage)
  • Major structural damage or broken frames
  • Severe water damage or mold
  • Ripped/torn upholstery beyond repair
  • Sleeper sofas with broken mechanisms
  • Heavily soiled or unsanitary pieces
